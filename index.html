Welcome to Panna Spin Equipments Pvt. Ltd.

Mr. R.K.Shah started manufacturing textile spinning equipment by developing the very first cot grinding machine in the year 1969 and creating the brand which is now globally recognized as SAGAR. The wide experience and frequent overseas visits of Mr.Shah have been instrumental in making this company one of the leading manufacturer and exporter of textile spinning and maintenance equipment all over the world. As a reputed manufacturer and exporter, we have been regularly participating in exhibitions both domestically and internationally. All our products have an authorized "CE" marking. With zero defects, committed delivery, after-sales services and technical support we have successfully supplied more than 500 sets of our complete range of products in almost all the textile spinning units in India as well as about 25 countries in the international market. We are having highly qualified R&D division who works for improving the products and developing the new technology for the benefit of good quality of yarn with full automation.
